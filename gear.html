Gear for climbing

Essential Climbing Equipment

Climbing Harness

A climbing harness is worn around the waist and legs to secure the climber to the rope. It distributes weight during falls and allows safe belaying and rappelling.

Ropes & Carabiners

Dynamic ropes stretch to absorb falls, while carabiners are metal loops with gates used to connect ropes, harnesses, and protection gear securely.

Climbing Shoes

Specialized shoes with sticky rubber soles that provide grip on rock surfaces and allow precise foot placements on small footholds.

Helmet

A helmet protects your head from falling rocks and impacts during climbs. Essential for outdoor and trad climbing safety.

Chalk & Chalk Bag

Chalk keeps hands dry and improves grip on holds. It is stored in a small bag attached to the climber's harness for easy access.

Belay Devices

Used to control the rope during belaying. They allow the belayer to manage slack, catch falls, and lower climbers safely.
