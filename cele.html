Climbing Celebrities

Jason Momoa

Known for his role as Aquaman, Jason is a passionate rock climber and even designs climbing gear. He often trains on indoor walls and bouldering gyms.

Tom Cruise

Famous for doing his own stunts, Tom climbed parts of Dead Horse Point in Utah for *Mission: Impossible 2*. He's known to practice on real rock faces.

Chris Sharma

One of the most influential rock climbers in the world. He's not just a celeb — he’s a legend in sport climbing and deep water soloing.

Alex Honnold

Best known for free-soloing El Capitan without a rope in *Free Solo*. A true climbing icon featured in media worldwide.

Alain Robert

Nicknamed the "French Spider-Man", Alain scales skyscrapers without ropes. He's globally known for urban climbing stunts.

More Climbers?

Other celebrities like Jared Leto, Brie Larson, and even Barack Obama have tried climbing — showing the sport’s growing popularity.
