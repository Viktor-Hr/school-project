Climbing Holds & Techniques

Jug

Large, deep holds that allow full hand grip. Perfect for beginners and overhangs. Pull with confidence using your full palm.

Crimp

Tiny ledges for fingertips. Requires finger strength and caution to avoid injury. Keep your body close to the wall.

Sloper

Rounded and smooth. Slopers use friction more than grip. Keep open hands and stable footwork to stay balanced.

Pinch

Held by squeezing with fingers and thumb. Engage your core and maintain pressure to avoid slipping.

Pocket

Small holes for one or two fingers. Use caution and avoid overloading small fingers. Precision is key.

Edge

Flat, narrow ledges. Requires accuracy and strong footwork. Best combined with tension and balance control.
